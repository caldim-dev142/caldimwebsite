## 🏗️ Core Product Pillars

### Integrated Time & Attendance Tracking
#### Your project logs, unified
spreadsheet-style weekly grids allow employees to log hours against assigned projects effortlessly.

#### Precise leave & holiday lock
Automatically sync approved leave requests and public holidays directly into timesheets. No manual double-entry.

#### Automated approval workflows
Managers review submissions instantly on compliance dashboards with one-click approval or rejection comments.

---

### Automated Payroll Engine
#### Direct timesheet-to-payroll pipeline
Approved hours feed directly into payroll runs. Loss of Pay (LOP) calculations are computed automatically.

#### Formula-driven salary structures
Cascading calculation support. Set percentage-based rules (e.g., `Basic = 40% CTC`, `HRA = 40% of Basic`) and let the platform resolve the hierarchy.

#### Compliance-locked calculations
Automatic computation of Provident Fund (PF), Employee State Insurance (ESI), Professional Tax (PT), and TDS under both old and new tax regimes.
